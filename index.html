About Me


I am a web and graphic designer capable of creating perfect logos, brand identity and websites. I have been freelancing for over 3 years in which I have gained much experience and knowledge to undertake major projects in graphics and website front-end development. I have a proven track record.



Expertise

I have extensive experience designing the front-end part of the website, different logos, branding campaigns, business cards, letterheads, posters and more.



Tool knowledge

To create high quality designs efficiently, I am able to use tools like Adobe Photoshop, Pixellab (smartphone), Photopea (Both on the desktop and smartphone), Vector Ink, Figma (For the UI/ UX Designs), Visual Studio Code (For writing codes on the desktop), Trebedit (smartphonecode editor) and resources like Canva and Logo Maker.



Passion

I am very passionate about seeing others succeed and creating simple, clear and high quality visuals from the vision of my client's makes me feel fulfilled.
